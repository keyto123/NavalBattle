Board size will change the n of nxn board
Boat Quantity is the quantity of smallest boat

Difficulty is the CPU level settings as of:

  • Easy: Only random attacks
  • Medium: Try to keep hitting the last hit boat
  • Hard: Search for boats hit from powers
  • Very Hard: Use minimum amount of hits in every boat

Notes: Every higher difficulty uses lower difficulty goodies

Powers that are given when your boat is defeated:

  • None: Received from small boat, common attack setting
  • Cross: Received from medium boat, make a cross with the center at clicking position
  • Square: Received from large boat, make a square with the center at clicking position

Wins who first destroy every enemy's boats. Have Fun!
